Chicken Sticks Recipe|

INGREDIENTS


Yield:4 servings
✓ 6 boneless skinless chicken breasts
✓ 1 cup apple cider vinegar
✓ ½ cup canola oil
✓ ¼ cup orange juice
✓ ¼ cup Worcestershire sauce
✓ 2 Tbsp Montreal Chicken seasoning
✓ 2 Tbsp sugar
✓ 1 Tbsp chili powder
✓ 1 Tbsp garlic powder

PREPARATION

Step 1:

Whisk together cider vinegar, oil, orange juice, Worcestershire sauce,
Montreal Chicken seasoning, sugar, chili powder, and garlic powder.

Step 2:

Place chicken in a ziplock bag, pound to an even thickness. Pour marinade over chicken and refrigerate a
few hours to overnight.

Step 3:

Remove chicken from marinade. Discard marinade.

Step 4:

Prepare grill and cook chicken for 12 to 15 minutes, until no longer pink.
