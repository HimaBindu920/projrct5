Mutton Curry Recipe|

INGREDIENTS


Yield:4 servings
✓ 1 clove garlic, peeled
✓ 1 (2 inch) piece fresh ginger root, peeled
✓ ¼ cup vegetable oil
✓ 1 (3 inch) cinnamon stick
✓ ½ teaspoon cumin seeds
✓ 1 star anise pod
✓ 8 makrut lime leaves
✓ 2 pounds mutton, cubed
✓ 10 shallots, sliced
✓ 2 large tomatoes, chopped
✓ 5 tablespoons curry powder
✓ ½ cup coconut milk
✓ salt to taste
✓ 4 cups water

PREPARATION

Step 1:

Combine the garlic and ginger in a food processor. Blend into a paste and set aside.

Step 2:

Heat the oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the cinnamon stick, cumin seeds, anise pod, and lime leaves.

Step 3:

Add the shallots; cook and stir until starting to brown, about 5 minutes. Stir in the ginger garlic paste.

Step 4:

Cook and stir for a few more minutes, and then stir in the mutton and tomatoes. Cover and simmer over low heat for 30 minutes.

Step 5:

Stir in the curry powder, coconut milk and water. Season with salt to taste.

Step 6:

Cover and simmer until mutton is tender, about 30 more minutes.
