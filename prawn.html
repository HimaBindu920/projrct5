Prawn Curry Recipe|

INGREDIENTS


Yield:4 servings
✓ 60 ml extra-virgin olive oil
✓ 60 ml lime juice
✓ 4 garlic cloves, crushed
✓ 3 tbsp. honey
✓ 2 tbsp. soy sauce
✓ 1 tbsp. chilli garlic sauce or Sriracha
✓ 900 g prawns, peeled and deveined
✓ Freshly chopped coriander, for garnish
✓ Lime wedges, for serving

PREPARATION

Step 1:

In a medium bowl, whisk together olive oil, lime juice, garlic, honey,
soy sauce, and chilli sauce.

Step 2:

Reserve 60ml marinade for brushing onto prawns while grilling.In a large bowl, toss prawns
with remaining marinade.

Step 3:

Preheat grill or grill pan and thread prawns onto skewers. Grill prawns until pink and opaque, about 3 minutes per side,
Garnish with coriander and serve hot with lime wedges.brushing with reserved marinade before and after each flip.

Step 4:

Garnish with coriander and serve hot with lime wedges.
