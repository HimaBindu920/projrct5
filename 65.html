Chicken 65 Recipe|

INGREDIENTS


Yield:4 servings
✓ 1/4 cup ketchup
✓ 1 tablespoon soy sauce
✓ 2 tablespoons white vinegar
✓ 1 drop red food colouring, optional
✓ 1 dash hot sauce, optional
✓ 2 tablespoons canola oil or other flavourless oil,cornstarch
✓ 2 teaspoons black mustard seeds,cumin powder,coriander powder
✓ 2 sprigs curry leaves,green chillies, split in half
✓ 1 cup oil for deep frying
✓ 3 pounds boneless chicken breast, in 1" cubes
✓ 1 teaspoon finely chopped garlic,ginger
✓ 1 teaspoon turmeric,red chiili powder/cayenne

PREPARATION

Step 1:

Mix the boneless chicken pieces with all the ingredients in marinade one.
Leave to marinate for a few hours but at least 30 minutes.

Step 2:

Heat a frying pan filled with enough oil to deep fry. Heat on medium high and have the oil hot to brown the
chicken quickly without cooking all the way through.

Step 3:

Have a bowl ready nearby with the ingredients for marinade 2. I don’t use the red Have a bowl
ready nearby with the ingredients for marinade 2. I don’t use the red colouring but the Chicken 65 is supposed to be a
searing, red colour. It’s up to you. The hot sauce is optional too, I don’t use it but if you like an extra kick, go ahead and add it.

Step 4:

Quickly fry the chicken pieces till brown and crispy but still slightly underdone. Remove the cooked pieces
to the bowl with marinade 2. Continue to fry all the pieces and add to the bowl.

Step 5:

Mix the marinade 2 into the cooked chicken pieces. Set aside as you heat the tempering oil.

Step 6:

Heat another fry pan on medium high heat and add the 2 tablespoons of oil. When the oil is hot, add the mustard seeds,
curry leaves and the green chillies.When the spluttering subsides a bit, and you can smell the mustard seeds,
drain the chicken from marinade 2 and add to the pan. Stir fry the chicken till the pieces are almost dry, cooked
and crispy. Remove to a serving dish.
